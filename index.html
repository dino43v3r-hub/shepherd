Privacy-first pastoral reflection

Shepherd

Write out an issue, concern, grief, spiritual struggle, or question. Shepherd turns it into a structured Christian reflection and pastoral care plan.

Your reflection is not saved. Everything stays in this browser session. No login, no database, no AI API.

Shepherd is a preparation and reflection tool. It is not a replacement for a pastor, priest, counselor, doctor, emergency service, or trusted human relationship.

Before you begin

Shepherd is for prayerful reflection and preparation for human care. It is not emergency help, counseling, medical care, or a substitute for a pastor, priest, doctor, therapist, or trusted person.

If you may be in immediate danger, might harm yourself or someone else, or are facing abuse or violence, contact emergency services or a local crisis service now. In the United States, call or text 988 for suicide or crisis support.
